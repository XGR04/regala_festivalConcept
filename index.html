Day 1

10am - 6pm

The festival kicks off with a vibrant cultural celebration, featuring performances by Palestinian artists, musicians, and dancers, highlighting the rich cultural tapestry of Palestine.

Day 2

May 18, 2024

10am - 6pm

The second day includes documentary screenings on Palestinian life and colonization, followed by Q&As with filmmakers and experts.

Day 3

May 19, 2024

The final day is a celebration concert featuring performances by renowned Palestinian artists and musicians, concluding the festival with a night of music and unity.

Purpose

The ongoing war between Israelis and Palestinians has brought about hardship and inequality, affecting people worldwide. The effort to defend their territory has caused many casualties for the Palestinians. Even though we live in the modern-day, land colonization is still an issue. CARE is putting up a lot of effort to support these communities by offering essential services like healthcare, education, and financial assistance. To build just and strong communities that uphold the dignity and rights of all people, it is critical to increase awareness of these problems and foster understanding.

Current Events

Current efforts are focused on rebuilding and providing immediate relief to affected communities. This includes the restoration of infrastructure, providing medical aid, and supporting local economies through various initiatives.
